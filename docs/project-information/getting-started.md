## Creating an Account

To purchase a server on **Fire Hosting**, you must first create an account.

1. Go to [**https://fire-hosting.net**](https://fire-hosting.net/)
2. Click on **Sign Up / Register**
3. Fill in the required information and validate your account

Once your account is created, you can log in and access all Fire Hosting services.
